### Introduction
Sarasvati is an Autonomous Surface Vehicle (ASV) program designed as a research and development BarunastraITS team in maritime competitions, including the International Roboboat Competition (IRC), Njord Challenge, and Kontes Kapal Cepat Tak Berawak Indonesia (KKI). This program focuses on the development of navigation technology, control systems, and obstacle avoidance.
